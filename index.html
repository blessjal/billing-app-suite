✅ E&M Billing Checklist
Check items that apply — billing level, payer estimates, and alerts update in real time on the right
1 — Problems & diagnoses —
Select the highest level that applies
Minimal / self-limited problem
Suture removal, wound check, prescription refill
Low — stable chronic illness(es)
Well-controlled HTN, DM, hyperlipidemia; minor acute illness
Moderate — chronic illness with exacerbation / new problem requiring workup
Crohn's flare, diverticulitis, new colorectal polyps, IBD management change
High — severe chronic illness / acute threat to life or function
Colorectal cancer staging, bowel obstruction, new cancer dx, sepsis, abscess
2 — Data reviewed & ordered —
Category A — Tests, documents, history (check all that apply)
✓
Reviewed and/or ordered labs
CBC, CMP, CEA, tumor markers, cultures, etc.
✓
Reviewed and/or ordered imaging / pathology
CT, MRI, PET, colonoscopy pathology, endoscopy results
✓
Reviewed external records / history from other provider
Outside hospital records, prior surgeon notes, oncology records
✓
Obtained history from someone other than patient
Family member, caregiver, interpreter-assisted history
Category B — Independent interpretation
✓
Independently interpreted test not reported by another provider
Personally reviewed the CT scan or pathology slide, not just the report
Category C — Discussion with other providers
✓
Discussion / consultation with external provider about management
Tumor board, GI consult, oncology discussion, anesthesia pre-op
3 — Risk of complications / morbidity —
Select the highest risk level that applies
Minimal — OTC medications, minor surgery, no risk factors
Hemorrhoid cream, stool softeners, suture removal
Low — OTC/prescription drugs, minor surgery with no risk factors
Fiber supplement, topical nitroglycerin, scheduling colonoscopy
Moderate — prescription drug management, minor surgery with risk factors, new diagnosis
Starting biologics for Crohn's, scheduling LAR or colectomy, managing post-op complications
High — drug therapy with intensive monitoring, major surgery with risk factors, hospitalization
Cancer treatment decisions, emergency surgery, complex multi-system management, bowel obstruction
4 — Time-based billing (alternative to MDM) —
Enter total time including face-to-face and care coordination on the date of the encounter.
5 — Modifiers 0 modifiers
Modifier 25 — Same-day procedure
✓
Procedure performed today AND E&M was significant and separately identifiable
Flex sig, anoscopy, botox injection, lesion removal at same visit. E&M must go beyond routine pre/post-procedure assessment.
Modifier 57 — Decision for major surgery
✓
Decision for major surgery (90-day global) made at this visit
Consented and scheduled for colectomy, LAR, APR, ostomy reversal. Document the decision discussion.
Post-operative modifiers
✓
Modifier 24 — Post-op visit for unrelated problem (in global period)
Problem completely unrelated to the surgery. Must document clearly.
✓
Modifier 58 — Staged or planned procedure during global period
Planned second-stage procedure, ostomy takedown after initial resection
✓
Modifier 79 — Unrelated procedure during global period
Procedure unrelated to the original surgery
Shared / split visit (Medicare)
✓
Modifier SA — NP/PA services, bill under supervising physician NPI
Physician must personally perform or supervise the substantial portion of the visit
Telemedicine modifiers
✓
Modifier 95 — Synchronous telemedicine (commercial / most payers)
Real-time audio/video. Document patient location, consent, and platform.
✓
Modifier GT — Telemedicine via interactive audio/video (Medicare)
Medicare-specific. Document patient consent and HIPAA-compliant platform.
6 — Documentation requirements 0 / 6
✓
Chief complaint and HPI documented
Onset, location, duration, character, associated signs, modifying factors, context
✓
Assessment and plan documents each problem addressed
Each diagnosis listed with corresponding plan — supports MDM complexity
✓
Data reviewed is explicitly named in the note
"Reviewed CT abdomen from 3/10/26 showing…" not just "reviewed imaging"
✓
Total time documented (if using time-based billing)
"Total time spent: 35 minutes including counseling and care coordination"
✓
Counseling content documented (if time spent counseling)
What was discussed, patient questions answered, understanding confirmed
✓
Physician signature, date, and attestation (for shared visits)
Shared visits: "I personally examined the patient and agree with the above assessment and plan"
